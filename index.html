GDPR, CCPA & PIPEDA Rights

This page explains your specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU), the California Consumer Privacy Act / CPRA, and Canada's PIPEDA — and exactly how to exercise them.

GDPR — European Users

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights under Regulation (EU) 2016/679:

Art. 15Right of Access — Obtain a copy of your personal data and information about how it is processed.
Art. 16Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate or complete incomplete personal data.
Art. 17Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your data where there is no compelling reason for continued processing.
Art. 18Right to Restrict Processing — Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
Art. 20Right to Data Portability — Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
Art. 21Right to Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.
Art. 22Automated Decision-Making — Not be subject to solely automated decisions with significant effects.

Our Data Protection contact: privacy@resumelab.com. You also have the right to complain to your national supervisory authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, CNIL in France, Datatilsynet in Denmark).

CCPA / CPRA — California Users

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act:

KnowKnow what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell).
DeleteRequest deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
CorrectRequest correction of inaccurate personal information.
Opt-OutOpt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share data.
LimitLimit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information.
Non-DiscriminationNot be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@resumelab.com with subject line "CCPA Request". We will respond within 45 days.

PIPEDA — Canadian Users

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to challenge its accuracy. We are accountable for the personal information under our control and comply with the 10 fair information principles under PIPEDA.

Contact our Privacy Officer: privacy@resumelab.com. You may also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca.

Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Enterprise customers requiring a formal Data Processing Addendum are invited to request one at privacy@resumelab.com. Our DPA covers:

  • Processing instructions and scope
  • Security measures (encryption, access controls)
  • Sub-processor list and obligations
  • Data breach notification (within 72 hours of awareness)
  • Data subject rights assistance
  • Deletion / return of data upon termination
